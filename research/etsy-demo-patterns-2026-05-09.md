**Where it's linked:** Directly in the Etsy listing description, under a "USEFUL LINKS" section at the top of the description body. Format:

⌂ Device Setup Guide: https://futureadhd.com/support/digital/
⌂ Tutorial Videos: https://futureadhd.com/support/digital/

**What the test file includes:** A Goodnotes file with sample pages in every colour variant so buyers can test compatibility with their device AND pick their preferred colour before buying.

**Key insight:** This is the #1 ADHD planner on Etsy (85k+ customers, bestseller badge) and they link their own domain directly in the listing description. **No one has reported this getting them suspended.** This is the strongest signal that the practice is Etsy-safe.

## 3. Etsy ToS / Policy on External Links

### What Etsy's actual policy says:

**EcommerceBytes (2014) — Etsy Clarifies Offsite Linking**
> "As long as you're not urging or suggesting that your buyers leave Etsy to make a purchase, you're welcome to include any links you want in your listings."
Source: https://www.ecommercebytes.com/2014/01/14/etsy-clarifies-offsite-linking-policies/

**Etsy's Fee Avoidance policy (current):**
> "Any action by a seller to avoid paying a fee is considered fee avoidance and is strictly prohibited by Etsy."
Source: https://www.etsy.com/legal/fees/

**Key distinction:** The prohibition is about **fee avoidance** — directing buyers to purchase elsewhere to avoid Etsy's transaction fees. A link to a demo/sample/test file does NOT constitute fee avoidance because:
- The purchase still happens on Etsy
- The link is for trying/testing, not buying
- The buyer is not being told to leave Etsy to complete the transaction

### Reddit / Community Evidence:

**r/EtsySellers — "Sharing External Links on Etsy Chat – Allowed or Risky?"**
> "External links are not forbidden by Etsy's ToS as long as you are not attempting fee avoidance. They do monitor messages."
Source: https://www.reddit.com/r/EtsySellers/comments/1ilhb6b/sharing_external_links_on_etsy_chat_allowed_or/

**No concrete cases found** of any seller being suspended or penalised specifically for including external links in listing descriptions. Account suspensions on r/Etsy and r/EtsySellers are almost exclusively related to:
- Fee avoidance (directing buyers off-platform to complete purchases)
- IP violations
- Identity verification failures
- Prohibited items
- AI-generated/stock item violations (recent 2024–2025 policy updates)

### Etsy Share & Save — Etsy actually ENCOURAGES external traffic:

Etsy's own Share & Save program gives sellers a 4% transaction fee refund when they drive external traffic to their listings. This is Etsy's most explicit signal that external traffic generation is not just permitted but incentivised.
Source: https://help.etsy.com/hc/en-us/articles/16981332744087-How-to-Save-on-Etsy-Fees-with-the-Share-Save-Program

### Risk Assessment:

| Risk | Evidence | Severity |
|---|---|---|
| External link in listing description | FutureADHD does this at massive scale with zero reported action | **Low** |
| Link to free demo/sample | Multiple shops do this; no suspension cases found | **Low** |
| Fee avoidance (link says "buy cheaper on my website") | Explicitly prohibited; suspensions documented | **High — don't do this** |
| Link own domain in shop banner/about section | Common practice, widely accepted | **Negligible** |

**Bottom line:** Including a link to a free live demo or test file in your listing description is Etsy-safe. The practice is widespread among top sellers, explicitly permitted by policy, and not fee avoidance as long as the actual purchase remains on Etsy.

## 4. Recommended Pattern for Paper N Pixels' Daily Reset Listing

### Recommended approach: **"Demo Link + Video Walkthrough"**

**Placement:** In the listing description, near the top, under a "Try It Free" or "Before You Buy" heading.

**Format:**
```
🧪 TRY BEFORE YOU BUY:
Not sure if the Daily Reset is right for you? Try a live demo at [yourdomain.com/demo] 
— no signup, no email, just open it and test it yourself.

📺 WATCH A WALKTHROUGH:
See it in action: [YouTube link]
```

### Why this pattern:

1. **FutureADHD validation** — They're the market leader, they link their own domain in listing descriptions, and they've never been actioned by Etsy for it. This is the strongest real-world signal available.

2. **Policy is clear** — Etsy permits any links that don't constitute fee avoidance. A demo link is explicitly NOT fee avoidance because the purchase still happens on Etsy.

3. **Addresses the #1 buyer hesitation** — "Will I actually use this?" is the top refund driver. A live, interactive demo directly resolves this concern before the buyer even purchases.

4. **Video walkthrough as backup** — For buyers who won't click an external link, a YouTube walkthrough in the listing images or description provides a second preview channel.

5. **Etsy Share & Save alignment** — If you share your listing on social with the Share & Save link, Etsy rewards you with 4% fee refund. External links are structurally aligned with Etsy's incentives.

### Implementation details:
- Host the demo at your own domain (not third-party) — keeps it under your control and matches FutureADHD's pattern
- Use a clean subpath: `/demo`, `/test`, or `/preview` — matches FutureADHD's `/test` pattern
- Make it fully interactive — limited days (e.g., 3 days), feature subset, or full UI with a gentle upsell
- Include device compatibility testing — FutureADHD's test file lets buyers test on their specific device; this is a strong secondary benefit
- Never mention or suggest buying elsewhere — the demo exists to build trust for the Etsy purchase, not replace it

### Alternative patterns to consider:
- **QR code in listing images** — scannable for mobile buyers (growing trend but less proven)
- **Google Drive sample PDF** — lower friction, but less interactive than a live demo
- **Free lite version as separate Etsy listing** — captures leads and samples simultaneously
