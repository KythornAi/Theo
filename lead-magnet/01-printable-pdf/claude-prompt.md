## What it is

A single-page PDF that gives ADHD buyers a taste of the Daily Reset guided flow — but on paper. It's not "another planner page." It's a one-shot reset tool for a bad day.

## What to build

Claude — please build a single A4 printable PDF. Design it for:
- **Paper N Pixels brand colours** — warm linen (#DFD3B8) bg with dark text, subtle earthy accents
- **Minimalist** — no headers, no dates, no guilt triggers
- **One-side only** — flip it over when done, literally close the day

### Layout (top to bottom):

**Title area:** "The Daily Reset" in the PNP wordmark style. Subtitle: "A one-page reset for when your brain won't cooperate."

**Section 1 — Mood check:** 4 small circles: Overwhelmed · Tired · Okay · Energised. Tick one. (Reuses the 4-mood scale from the v2 plan.)

**Section 2 — Brain dump box:** A lined box with the prompt "Get it out of your head. No filters, no structure, just dump it here." Takes up ~30% of the page. Includes a small note: "Keep going. There's no wrong answer."

**Section 3 — The One Thing:** A single text line (dotted) with the prompt "What's the ONE thing you need to get done today? Just one. That's enough."

**Section 4 — Survival basics:** 4 tiny checkboxes in a row with no labels — just icons: 💧 (water) 🍽️ (food) 🚿 (hygiene) 🫂 (connect). The prompt: "The bare minimum exists. Tick what you've done."

**Footer — The close-out:** A line: "That's today. You did enough. Rip this page out, fold it, and put it away. Tomorrow is fresh." Below: "Made by an ADHDer for ADHD brains" + papernpixelshop.com + a small "Get the interactive Daily Reset →" link.

### Design constraints:
- Must print on A4 with no bleed
- Must work in B&W if no colour printer
- No dates, no streaks, no scoring
- Font: system sans-serif (loads on any device)
- File format: HTML+CSS → print to PDF (or Canva export)
- Export as print-ready PDF
