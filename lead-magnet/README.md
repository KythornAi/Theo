---

## Why a lead magnet?

The PRODUCT_SPEC (§Lead Magnet) already calls for one: "Free 1-page ADHD Daily Reset PDF template." But we haven't designed which one yet. Here's what it needs to do:

1. **Capture emails** — Gumroad freebie with email gate, or ConvertKit/Kit form
2. **Taste the Daily Reset experience** — must preview or demo the core product
3. **Solve one real ADHD pain point** — not a generic "productivity tips" PDF
4. **Low friction to create** — we don't want to spend weeks on a freebie

---

## Pricing ladder it feeds

```
FREE lead magnet  ──→  Low Energy Pack (£5)  ──→  Weekly Reset (£7)  ──→  Daily Reset (£15)  ──→  Bundle (£24)
```

The lead magnet should feel like a genuine help, not a sales pitch. Trust builds the ladder.

## Decision framework

When Kyle reviews, weigh:

- **Time to ship** — how fast can we get this live?
- **Audience match** — does this attract Daily Reset buyers specifically?
- **Email capture** — does it naturally ask for an email?
- **Reuse potential** — can it be re-skinned / updated easily?
- **Sales conversion** — does it naturally lead to the paid product?

My recommendation is at the bottom of this doc.

---

## My recommendation: Lead with Option 2 (Mini HTML tool)

**Why:**
- Direct taste of Daily Reset — highest conversion potential
- Low effort (1-screen HTML, can reuse design system from product)
- Unique in the niche — no competitor offers a live interactive freebie
- Easy to host on papernpixelshop.com/demo or GitHub Pages
- Feeds both email capture AND live demo trust

**But start with Option 1 (PDF) as the quick win** — it can ship in a day while Option 2 takes a bit more design care. Option 1 captures emails. Option 2 converts them.

**Option 3 (Notion)** only if Kyle has or wants a Notion audience — it's a different platform from Etsy.

**Options 4 and 5** are post-launch refinements. Not for pre-launch.
