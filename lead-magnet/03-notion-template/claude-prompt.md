## What it is

A free Notion template that replicates the core Daily Reset flow in Notion-native format. One page. A daily check-in + brain dump + task list. No complex databases, no dashboards, no stats.

## Why Notion?

Notion has a huge ADHD audience (it's a common recommendation on r/ADHD). A Notion template lead magnet captures the tech-savvy ADHD user who likes systems but hasn't tried our HTML tool yet. It also opens a different distribution channel — Notion template galleries and Gumroad.

## What to build

Claude — please build a Notion template. Describe the structure so it can be built in Notion manually.

### Template structure (one page):

**Page title:** "Daily Reset — [date]" (auto-filled)

**Section 1 — Check-in:** 
- "How are you right now?" with 4 toggle options (Overwhelmed / Tired / Okay / Energised)
- Each toggle contains a short comfort message. Example: Overwhelmed toggle contains "Start with one breath. Then just one thing. That's it."

**Section 2 — Brain Dump:**
- A simple bullet list block with the prompt "Everything in your head. No filter. Just dump."
- Below: "Got it out? Great. Let's pick one."

**Section 3 — The One Thing:**
- A checkbox with the label "Today's one thing: ___"
- Below: "That's your win for today. Everything else is bonus."

**Section 4 — Survival Check:**
- 4 checkboxes with emoji labels only: 💧 ☕ 🍽️ 🚶
- No text descriptions — the icons speak for themselves

**Section 5 — Close-Out:**
- A quote block: "You did what you could today. That's enough."
- A button to duplicate the template for tomorrow (or just close the page)

### Design notes:
- No databases (too complex for a freebie)
- No linked views
- No formulas
- Simple colours: warm tones matching PNP brand
- Use Notion's callout blocks for the mood prompts
- Include a small footer: "Made by Paper N Pixels — ADHD tools that work with your brain, not against it. [link]"

### Automation tip:
Set up the template so the "Tomorrow" button duplicates the page with the next day's date already applied, clearing the brain dump and task sections.
