# Content Calendar — Pre-Launch Pinterest Strategy

> **Principle:** Pinterest is a search engine with a 3-month lead time (fairyfoxdigital.com).
> Start pinning BEFORE launch, not after. By launch day, pins should already be ranking.

---

## Pre-Launch Phase (now → Etsy listing live)

**Duration:** 4-6 weeks before Etsy launch
**Goal:** Build search presence + lead magnet capture before product goes live

### Week 1-2: Foundation
| Task | What | Why |
|------|------|-----|
| Set up Pinterest business account | PaperNPixelShop name, bio, link to lead magnet landing page | Required for analytics |
| Create boards (see 03-pin-strategy.md) | 6 boards matching content pillars | Organise content for Pinterest algorithm |
| Keyword research | Use Pinterest Trends tool to validate keywords | Ensure pins rank |
| Write 2 blog posts | Pick 2 topics from 02-blog-topics.md | Long-form content to pin from |
| Build lead magnet landing page | One page on papernpixelshop.com | Destination for all pins |

### Week 3-4: First Pins
| Task | What | Details |
|------|------|---------|
| Create 10 pins | 2 per blog post + 2 lead magnet pins | Mix of Template A, B, C |
| Schedule 1 pin/day | Pinterest free scheduler (100 pins/mo) | Consistent daily posting |
| First Pinterest ad test? | Only if budget allows — organic first | Skip if unsure |
| Add 5 pins to group boards | Find ADHD/productivity group boards | Wider reach |

### Week 5-6: Ramp Up
| Task | What | Details |
|------|------|---------|
| Write 2 more blog posts | Fill content gaps | More pins to create |
| Create 10-15 more pins | New angles on existing content | Fresh pins for same blog posts |
| Schedule 2 pins/day | Increase cadence | Pinterest favours consistent posting |
| Monitor analytics | Which pins get impressions/clicks | Double down on what works |

## Launch Week

| Day | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Day 1 | Etsy listing live. Pin product mockup + demo link to "Paper N Pixels — Tools" board |
| Day 2 | Blog post: "The Daily Reset is live" + pins to all boards |
| Day 3 | Email to lead magnet subscribers: product launch |
| Day 4 | Fresh pin batch — seasonal/trending angle |
| Day 5-7 | Monitor clicks, adjust keywords, repin best performers |

---

## Pinterest Cadence Summary

| Metric | Pre-launch | Launch | Post-launch |
|--------|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Pins/day | 1-2 | 2-3 | 3-5 |
| Blog posts/week | 1 | 1 | 2 |
| Lead magnet landing page | 1 | Updated with product links | Maintained |
| Product pins | 0 | 5-7 | 2-3/week |
| Analytics check | Weekly | Daily first week, then weekly | Weekly |

## First 90-day goal

- **500 Pinterest followers** on PaperNPixelShop account
- **50-100 email subscribers** from lead magnet
- **Blog posts indexed and ranking** in Pinterest search for 3+ keywords
- **Pins getting 1k+ monthly impressions** each for top 5 pins
- **First Etsy sale from Pinterest traffic** (validation of the funnel)

If these metrics aren't hit by 90 days, re-evaluate: are the keywords wrong, are the pins not compelling, or is the lead magnet not converting?
