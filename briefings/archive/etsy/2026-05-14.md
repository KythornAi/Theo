## "Daily Reset" Landscape

The "daily reset" angle is growing as a distinct sub-niche:

- **Multiple shops** now using "Daily Reset" in product names

- **Key observation:** The combination "daily reset + brain dump + focus sheet" is the most common pattern. Shops are packaging this as a standalone sheet, not a full planner. This is a gap — nobody is doing a full planner with daily reset as the core architecture yet.

## Spotted This:

**The "Daily Reset" architecture gap is still unclaimed by any major shop.** FutureADHD has a daily focus page (their most-praised feature) but doesn't market it as a "daily reset." The standalone daily reset products (single PDF sheets) prove there's demand, but nobody has built a full planner with daily reset as the organizing principle. This is our strongest differentiation opportunity — a planner where every spread centers on a reset/check-in/check-out flow rather than traditional time-blocking.
