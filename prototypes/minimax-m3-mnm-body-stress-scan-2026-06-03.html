The Body Stress Scan

A short, guided scan to notice where stress sits in your body. Not to fix it. Just to notice.

You can do this sitting or lying down, eyes open or closed. There is no right way. There is no keeping count. You can stop at any time.

You will be guided through three settling breaths, then asked, one area at a time, what you feel.

For each area, pick the word that fits best. If you feel nothing, that is a valid answer too.

Settle

In through the nose. Out through the mouth. Let your shoulders drop.
