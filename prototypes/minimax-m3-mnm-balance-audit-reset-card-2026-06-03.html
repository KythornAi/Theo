The Balance Audit + Reset Card

A two-column audit, one weekly adjustment, and a small card you can keep on you. Not a tracker. Not a streak. Just a piece of paper that says, when things tilt, here is what helps.

Takes about five minutes. Your draft is saved in this browser only, never sent anywhere.

If you would rather just see an example, you can also tap the chips inside the audit and we will prefill the card for you.

      Step 3 of 3

      Your Reset Card

      Keep this somewhere close

      Three lines, written down. When things tilt, you read it and you do the smallest next thing.

      Reset Card

      When things tilt

      1. 1

        Three breaths.

        In through the nose, out through the mouth. Slow, not deep.

      2. 2

        Name the tilt.

        "I'm anxious." "I'm exhausted." Just the word, not the story.

      3. 3

        One tiny steadying action.

        Pick the smallest good step and do it. Let that be the hinge.
