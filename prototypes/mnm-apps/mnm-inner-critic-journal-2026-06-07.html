The Inner Critic Journal.

Notice. Name. Respond with kindness.

The inner critic is loudest when we are already struggling. This is a short, calm space to write what is here, and then respond the way you would to a close friend.

There is no right way to do this. Just write what comes.

Step one

What is the inner critic saying right now?

It does not have to be fair or rational. Just write what it is saying.

Step two

This is a moment of difficulty.

Struggle is part of being human.

Where do you feel this in your body? Tap the area that holds it. If you cannot find a place, that is also fine.

Tap a region: forehead, jaw, shoulders, arms, chest, stomach, legs.

Step three

What would you say to a close friend feeling exactly this?

You do not need to be kind to yourself yet. Just write what you would say to someone you care about.
