An exercise for two.

Mindful Listening

Most conversations have two people talking. This one has two people listening. Three minutes each to share what is present, with no interruption, no advice, no fixing. Then a short joint reflection.

About ten minutes in total. A quiet space helps.

  • Two people
  • Ten minutes
  • A quiet space

The listener's only job is to stay present. No problem-solving, no reassurance, no rehearsing what to say next. Just witness.
