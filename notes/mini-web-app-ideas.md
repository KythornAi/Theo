#### "Habit-Stacked Recovery Menu" Builder — long-term resilience
- **Recovery Menu Categorizer:** drag/drop actions into Micro (<10m) or Macro (hours/days).
- **Implementation Intention Generator:** "When X, then Y" formula.
- **Negativity Bias Counter:** daily gratitude log — one thing plus three ripple effects.

### Mechanics worth reusing
- **Return Counter:** celebrate the return, not the streak; this is ADHD-native and avoids shame loops.
- **Implementation Intention Generator:** a high-conversion habit formula that converts intention into a specific trigger/action pair.
- **Body location tapping:** somatic check-in without clinical language.
- **Time-window selector:** "how much space?" respects energy and time variance.
