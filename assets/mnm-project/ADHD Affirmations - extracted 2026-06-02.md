## Flagged for a human / sense check

The deck is strong overall: practical, anti-toxic-positivity, ADHD-aware. Most lines are clean and human. These are the ones worth a second look before they go into the app. Nothing here is changed in the list above; these are suggestions for Kyle to approve or reject.

### 1. US spelling (brand is UK English)
The printed cards use US spelling in two places. The cards are already exported, so fixing them on the cards means a Canva re-export, but the **web app should use UK spelling** to match the brand voice.
- Card 40: "adds **color** to the world" -> "adds **colour** to the world"
- Card 52: "learning to **prioritize**" -> "learning to **prioritise**"

### 2. Borderline mystical phrasing (brand rule: no mystic framing)
- Card 33: "Creativity **flows naturally through me**." This leans slightly woo, which rubs against the "mindful, not mythical / no crystals or mysticism" positioning. Suggested rework: "I have a natural creative streak." or "Creativity comes easily to me."

### 3. Comma splices (style call, not an error)
Affirmation decks often join two clauses with a comma for rhythm. These read fine spoken, but if you want cleaner grammar in the app:
- Card 13: "Focus comes in waves, I trust mine will return." -> "Focus comes in waves. I trust mine will return."
- Card 31: "It's okay to need extra support, asking for help is strength." -> **APPROVED (Kyle 2026-06-02): "It's okay to need extra support. Asking for help shows strength."** (split the comma splice AND changed "is strength" to "shows strength", which holds the meaning more softly and naturally).
- Card 56: "Forgetting is natural, I can always reset." -> "Forgetting is natural. I can always reset." (this card also has no full stop on the print)

### 4. Mild cliche / could feel more human
- Card 35: "My energy brings life to the projects I touch." ("everything I touch" is a touch cliche). Optional rework: "I bring real energy to the things I care about."
- Card 39: "I can use my hyperfocus as a superpower when it serves me." ("superpower" is common in ADHD content but a little overused). Optional: "When my hyperfocus kicks in, I can use it well."

### 5. Thematic duplication (consider for variety in shuffle)
Several cards cover the same ground (restart / reset / starting over): cards 12, 18, 56, and partly 13/16. Not wrong, but in a shuffle previewer they can feel repetitive. Optional: swap one or two for fresh angles when we finalise.
