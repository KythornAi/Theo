Where does stress sit in you?

A short, guided scan. You will be asked to notice, one area at a time, what you feel. Not to fix it. Not to grade the answer. Just to look.

what you will do
  1. Three slow breaths to settle.
  2. Tap a body area. Pick a word that fits.
  3. Read the keepsake. Copy it if you want to keep it.

You can do this sitting or lying down. Eyes open or closed. There is no right way. There is no keeping count. You can stop at any time.
