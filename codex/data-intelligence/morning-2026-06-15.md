## General Trends

1. **AI integration gap is the money driver.** Companies are adopting AI tools (78%) but most lack the expertise to make them work well. Freelancers who bridge this gap — connecting tools, building workflows, coaching teams — are earning premiums. This is the biggest signal of 2026.

2. **Always-on content production is replacing project-based work.** Canva designers +403%, Instagram content +149%. Businesses need daily content, not quarterly brand refreshes. This creates upstream demand for research, topic ideation, and content strategy — areas Theo can serve.

3. **The global freelance market is past $1T+** (62M US freelancers, $1.35T contribution in 2025). Specialised freelancers earn 2-4x generalists. The premium is on proven expertise over credentials (74% of executives say degrees don't matter when hiring freelancers).

4. **Claude Code & AI Dev Tools are exploding.** +938% on Fiverr. This suggests businesses are hungry for AI-accelerated development but need human setup/management. Not a direct Theo play, but validates the overall AI-service market.
