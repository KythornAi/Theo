## Why Theo added extra angles

- The checker needs trust and positioning research, not just factual HMRC content, because tax-adjacent tools can feel risky if disclaimers and reassurance are weak.
- The site needs a monetisation/product map before build decisions, because the best route may be affiliate comparison, accountant lead-gen, email capture, checklists, calculators, or a combination.
- Traffic-channel and Pinterest/social scans were added on 2026-05-23 to test whether demand can be reached outside pure Google search and to avoid building a checker with no acquisition route.
