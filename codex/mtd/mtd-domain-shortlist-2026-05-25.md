# MTD Checker Domain Shortlist — 2026-05-25

## Summary

Domain-name brainstorming for the Making Tax Digital checker project. Initial favourite `MTDReady.co.uk` is not available and has nearby live/registered competitors, so the chosen domain is `ReadyForMTD.co.uk`.

Kyle checked Namecheap on 2026-05-25, saw `readyformtd.co.uk` available for **$6.98 for the year**, and later confirmed: **`ReadyForMTD.co.uk` is now ours.**

## Current Recommendation

1. **ReadyForMTD.co.uk** — chosen and purchased. Clear, friendly, plain-English, less collision risk than “MTD Ready”.
2. **CheckMTD.co.uk** — short, direct, tool-like. Good if we want a very functional checker brand.
3. **DigitalTaxCheck.co.uk** — broader and safer if the project may expand beyond MTD.
4. **IsMyMTDReady.co.uk** — strong question-led conversion domain, but still close to the crowded “MTD ready” wording.
5. **TaxReadyChecker.co.uk** — broader and safer, but weaker MTD-specific search intent.

## Competitor / Collision Notes

### `mtd-ready.co.uk`

Live site. Very close to our original concept:

- “Are you ready for Making Tax Digital?”
- free 60-second check
- threshold explainer
- MTD-compatible software recommendations
- affiliate-style links to FreeAgent, Xero, QuickBooks

The page title references “MTDReady.co.uk”, despite the live URL being `mtd-ready.co.uk`. This makes the `MTDReady` naming cluster too crowded for our project.

### `getmtdready.co.uk`

Live service-led site. Offers done-for-you MTD setup for landlords and sole traders:

- free consultation
- software sourcing/setup
- HMRC connection
- packages around £350 setup, £400 + £85/month, and £500 + £175/month

This validates demand but positions as high-touch service, not a lightweight self-serve checker.

### `mtddigitalsolutions.co.uk`

Live bookkeeping/accounting support site:

- MTD setup and migration
- ongoing bookkeeping
- MTD ITSA/VAT support
- software training
- consultation-led service

Again, more service/accountant style than our likely checker-plus-setup-pack route.

## Naming Guidance

Prefer:

- clear, plain-English, non-gimmicky names
- “ready”, “check”, “digital tax” language
- names that do not sound like HMRC or official compliance certification

Avoid/deprioritise:

- `audit` — scary and too formal
- `expert` — raises credibility/legal expectations
- `verify` — can sound official or identity-check related
- heavy `compliance` wording — may increase trust/legal burden
- exact “MTD Ready” cluster due to existing live competitors

## Final Decision

- **Chosen domain:** `ReadyForMTD.co.uk`
- **Decision status:** purchased/owned by Kyle as of 2026-05-25
- **Why this wins:** clear UK-facing MTD intent, plain-English trust signal, avoids the crowded `MTDReady`/`MTD Ready` naming cluster, and does not imply HMRC affiliation or official compliance certification.

## Next Step

Use `ReadyForMTD.co.uk` as the working public domain/brand for the MTD Checker project. Later build/domain setup should point DNS and launch copy around this name. Optional defensive buys to consider only if cheap enough: `ReadyForMTD.uk` or `ReadyForMTD.com`; `.co.uk` remains the priority.
