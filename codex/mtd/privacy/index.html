Privacy policy

The checker runs entirely in your browser. We do not store your answers, and we do not ask for personal identifiers.

What we collect

Your checker answers are not stored anywhere. All checker logic runs client-side in your browser. When you leave the page, your answers are gone. We do not send your answers to any server, and we do not use localStorage or cookies to save them.

We do not ask for your:

  • National Insurance number
  • UTR (Unique Taxpayer Reference)
  • HMRC login or Government Gateway details
  • Address or bank details

Email (optional, after your result only)

If you choose to opt in after your result, you can tick separate boxes for:

  • Email me this result (one-off service message)
  • Send me MTD deadline reminders (periodic service reminders)
  • Send me product updates and guides (marketing)

There are no pre-ticked boxes. Marketing consent is separate from result delivery and reminders. You can unsubscribe at any time. We follow ICO and PECR guidance on electronic marketing. If you do not opt in, we do not have your email address.

Analytics

We use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-friendly, cookie-light analytics tool. It does not use cookies and does not track individuals across sites. We track aggregate events only: checker start, checker complete, result state, email opt-in, and software card clicks. We do not use Google Analytics.

Affiliate links

Some software links on this site may be affiliate links in future. If you click one and sign up, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. This does not influence your MTD result, which is based on HMRC guidance and your answers. Affiliate disclosure appears near any affiliate content.

Your rights

You can contact us at any time to ask what data we hold, to request deletion, or to unsubscribe from emails. Because we do not store checker answers, there is no personal data to delete from the checker itself. If you have opted in to email, we hold your email address and your consent choices only.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: hello@readyformtd.co.uk.
