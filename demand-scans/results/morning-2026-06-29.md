## General Trends

1. **Specialisation over generalisation is now confirmed, not emerging.** Upwork's data shows "deep subject matter expertise is now more important to companies hiring freelancers" — generalists are losing. AI automation micro-agencies with vertical specialisation command 2-3x pricing.

2. **The "prototype to production" gap is the big opportunity.** Fiverr's expert insight: "Anyone can spin up a prototype now, but there's a massive gap between that and something that actually holds up." Businesses are discovering this the hard way. Freelancers who bridge experimentation to reliable production are the biggest winners.

3. **Human "power skills" increasingly valued as AI handles technical work.** Upwork reports demand for creative thinking, communication, problem-solving surging as companies integrate AI. The winning combination: technical capability + human judgment. Not either/or.
