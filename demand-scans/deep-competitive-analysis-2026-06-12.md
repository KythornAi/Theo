## What These Competitors ARE NOT Doing

1. **No one is offering a narrative business health report** — they all deliver dashboards or raw analysis. Nobody writes "here's what this means for your business" in natural language.

2. **No one is identifying blind spots** — dashboards show what you ask for. Nobody finds what you're missing.

3. **No one is doing cross-month trend analysis as a product** — it's always one-off or dashboard maintenance. Nobody says "your sales dip every September — here's why."

4. **No one targets the £97-197/month retainer range** — the market is split between $40-245 (Fiverr, basic dashboard maintenance) and $300-1,500 (Fiverr Pro/Upwork retainer) and $4,500+ (agencies). The £150-350 gap is wide open.

5. **No one is doing "done-for-you ecommerce insights" as a recurring PDF product** — it's always dashboards (self-serve) or consulting (interactive).

## The Unserved Gap (Our Positioning)

**The gap at £97-197/month:**

A business owner gets:
- A clean written report delivered to them monthly
- 3-5 things they didn't know about their business
- Trends they would have missed
- Plain English, not spreadsheet-speak
- Zero work on their part — send data, get insights

**Compared to the alternatives:**
- **Fiverr gigs ($40-245/mo):** You get a dashboard. You still have to read it yourself, interpret it, figure out what to do. No narrative.
- **Upwork freelancers ($25-50/hr):** You have to brief them, manage them, check their work. They're not proactive.
- **Agencies ($4,500-10k/mo):** Great but 20-50x our price. Most small businesses can't afford it.
- **AI tools (ChatGPT, etc.):** Can't access your actual data. Generic output.

**What we do that none of these do:** We read your data, write you a story about what's happening in your business, and point out what you're missing — every month, for less than the cost of a dinner out.

## Recommended First Niche: E-commerce (Shopify) Stores

**Why start here:**
1. Cleanest data pipeline — Shopify CSV exports are standardised
2. Owners already understand sales analytics value
3. They spend money on ads ($500-10k/mo) — a £147 report is pocket change
4. Monthly reporting is natural for ecommerce (monthly sales cycles)
5. Data is abundant — product performance, customer cohorts, seasonal trends, ad ROAS

**Sample first-client pitch:**
> "You have Shopify analytics. You may have Google Analytics and Triple Whale too. But nobody is reading all of them together and telling you what's actually happening in your business. Send us your monthly exports — we send back a 5-page report with 3-5 specific things to act on. £147/month."
