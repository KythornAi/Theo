## 🔍 Blind Spots Identified

These are things happening in your business data that you may not have noticed:

### 1. Revenue dropped -57.7% from 2011-11 to 2011-12. If this is a recurring seasonal pattern, the business should be preparing for it — not reacting to it.

**Impact:** Reactive management leads to stock issues, cash flow problems, and missed opportunities to run counter-seasonal promotions.

**Evidence in the data:** Largest month-over-month drops: 2011-11→2011-12 (-57.7%), 2011-03→2011-04 (-25.1%), 2011-01→2011-02 (-24.3%)

### 2. 13% of revenue comes from outside the UK (Net). If the business isn't actively optimizing for these markets, it's leaving money on the table.

**Impact:** Untapped growth lever. Existing export demand proves product-market fit outside the UK.

**Evidence in the data:** Export revenue: £1,344,246 across export markets

## ✅ Recommendations for This Month

### 1. Map the seasonal revenue pattern across all months. If the same months drop every year, plan counter-seasonal campaigns (loyalty bonuses, clearance events, bundled offers) to smooth the curve.

- **Effort:** Medium — needs historical data and planning, but no new tools
- **Expected impact:** Medium — flatter revenue curve means better cash flow and less panic

### 2. Analyse export markets individually — which countries have the highest average order value? Which have the best repeat rates? Consider localised marketing or targeted ad spend for the top 2-3 export markets.

- **Effort:** Low-medium — analysis is quick, localised marketing requires some investment
- **Expected impact:** Medium-high — growing existing export markets is cheaper than acquiring new UK customers

## Methodology

This report was generated from raw transactional data. The process was:

1. **Data cleaning** — removed cancelled orders (InvoiceNo starting with 'C'), negative quantities, returns, and rows with missing values
2. **Transaction analysis** — aggregated by month, product, customer, and country
3. **Pattern identification** — compared month-over-month, identified concentration risks, calculated repeat purchase rate
4. **Blind spot mapping** — cross-referenced findings against typical ecommerce benchmarks to flag what the business may be missing
5. **Recommendation generation** — each blind spot was matched with a specific actionable step
