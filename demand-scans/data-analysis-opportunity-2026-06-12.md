# Data Analysis & Reporting Lane — Opportunity Brief

**Date:** 2026-06-12
**Source:** Demand scan results + targeted research

## The Core Idea

**Recurring business data health reports.** A business sends their raw data (Shopify exports, sales CSVs, inventory dumps, whatever) — we return a polished report with key insights, blind spots they're missing, and actionable recommendations.

Pure deliverable. No install. No training. No site visits.

## Why This Works

### Market validation

- **332 active data cleaning jobs on Upwork** at time of snapshot (real demand, not theoretical)
- **Data analyst rates:** $25-50/h entry, $50-100/h mid, $100-200+/h senior
- **Project pricing:** $1,000-5,000 for single analysis, $5,000-15,000 for recurring
- **Data cleaning +210% YoY growth** on Upwork — one of the fastest-growing categories
- **Shopify analytics feel "incomplete"** (Reddit r/ecommerce) — user journeys, attribution, assisted conversions, cohort behaviour are missing or limited in native tools

### The gap

Existing tools give business owners **dashboards and charts**. What they don't give is:

- A human-readable narrative ("here's what changed this month and why it matters")
- Cross-metric insights no single dashboard connects ("your ad spend went up 20% but conversion rate dropped — here's where the leak is")
- Actionable blind-spot identification ("you're losing 15% of cart abandoners at the shipping page")
- Trend spotting across months ("this dip happens every September — are you ready for it next year?")

Shopify plugins like Triple Whale, Gorgias, and Conjura give them *more data*. They don't give them *someone who reads it and tells them what to do*.

## What a First Offer Looks Like

### One-off "Business Health Check" report

**What the client gets:**
- Cleaned and structured data summary
- 5-8 page report covering: sales trends, customer patterns, product performance, seasonality, anomalies
- 3-5 specific actionable recommendations
- Delivered as polished PDF

**Pricing:** £97-197

### Recurring "Monthly Analytics Package"

**What the client gets:**
- Monthly data take-on and cleaning
- Monthly report (same structure, evolving narrative)
- Quarter-over-quarter trend tracking
- Priority access for ad-hoc questions

**Pricing:** £97-197/mo

### Which businesses this works best for

1. **E-commerce stores** (Shopify, WooCommerce) — mountains of data, owners too busy to analyse it, they already understand the value of sales analytics
2. **Service businesses with recurring billing** — easy data exports, clear patterns to find
3. **Local businesses** — spreadsheet-based, no existing analytics setup, huge untapped opportunity

### What it does NOT require

- No Shopify app development
- No API integrations
- No cloud account setup
- No training staff
- No site visits
- No ongoing support beyond the report

## Market Positioning

The critical insight from the demand scans: **"Small businesses want execution, not advice."**

This report isn't advice — it's a deliverable. The client gets a tangible document they can read, share with their team, and act on. That's the difference between "data analysis consultant" (service, hands-on) and "monthly business health report" (product, self-contained).

## Competition Check

**Who else does this?**
- Individual freelance data analysts on Upwork/Fiverr ($25-200/h)
- Marketing agencies that include reporting as part of a retainer ($2,000-5,000/mo full-service)
- AI tools like Copy.ai, Jasper, etc. (generic, no real data processing)

**What we have that they don't:**
- Theo's automation means we can process data faster than a human analyst
- Claude's narrative quality beats any AI tool
- Kyle's business review adds the human layer that pure AI reports miss
- Our cost is lower than an agency but our quality is higher than a solo freelancer using spreadsheets

## Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| "I can just use ChatGPT for this" | ChatGPT can't access their raw data, can't clean it, can't run cross-month analysis. We deliver a processed product, not a prompt. |
| Too many competitors | Most competitors are either (a) expensive agencies or (b) cheap freelancers. We're neither — we're AI-powered but human-reviewed, which is a distinct lane. |
| Hard to find first clients | Start with one case study. Offer a free/cheap first report to a real business. Use the artifact to sell the next one. |
| Data security concerns | Reports are processed locally. Raw data is not stored after delivery. Clear privacy policy. |
