## Design Notes
**Color Palette** (ADHD-friendly, calming):
- Primary: Soft slate blue (#1E293B) - focus and trust
- Secondary: Muted sage green (#64748B) - balance and growth
- Accent: Gentle coral (#F59E0B) - attention and energy
- Background: Warm white (#F8FAFC) - reduced glare
- Text: Dark charcoal (#1E293B) - high readability

**Typography**:
- Headers: Rounded, friendly sans-serif
- Body: Clear, readable font with good spacing
- Labels: Slightly smaller but very clear

**Layout Flow**:
1. Title at top (small)
2. Central hub slightly below center
3. Five spokes radiating outward evenly
4. Each spoke with label, content, icon, and visual element
5. Ample whitespace between elements for visual clarity
6. Watercolor texture throughout for engaging, hand-made feel

## Pinterest Optimization
- Vertical 9:16 aspect ratio stands out in feed
- Clear visual hierarchy for quick scanning
- Minimal text, maximum visual communication
- Brand-consistent with Paper N Pixels identity
- Educational value drives saves and shares
- Faceless marketing-friendly (no people needed)
