# ADHD Task Breaker Concept — 2026-05-03

## Summary
Based on harvesting 25 Reddit pain points, 25 Etsy listings, and 20 Pinterest trends, the ADHD Task Breaker emerges as the top opportunity for a mini-web app. It addresses Decision Overwhelm, Planner Frustration, and Task Paralysis with high impact and high ease of implementation.

## Sources
- Reddit pain point harvest: 25 entries from r/ADHD, r/ADHDwomen, r/productivity (last 7 days)
- Etsy competitor analysis: 25 ADHD planner listings
- Pinterest trend review: 20 pins on ADHD planner topics

## Findings

### Top Validated ADHD Pain Points
1. **Time Blindness** (4 mentions) - Most impacting symptom
2. **Decision Overwhelm** (3 mentions) - Planning creates paralysis  
3. **Planner Frustration** - Systems fail when consistency breaks
4. **Task Paralysis** - Too many options → inability to start
5. **Hyperfocus Trap** - Losing track of time on wrong tasks

- **Printable/PDF Focus**: 10 listings  
- **Digital/Hyperlinked**: 13 listings

**1. ADHD Task Breaker** (Score: 9/10)
- Description: Guided task decomposition tool that breaks overwhelming projects into ADHD-friendly chunks with built-in momentum techniques and minimal decision points.
- Pain Points Addressed: Decision Overwhelm, Planner Frustration, Task Paralysis
- Etsy Market Gap: Etsy focuses on planning pages, not execution support for overwhelmed users
- Trend Alignment: Medium - consistent interest in productivity systems
- Impact: High | Ease: High

**2. Time Blindness Visualizer** (Score: 8/10)
- Description: Simple web tool that makes time tangible through visual timers, progress bars, and retrospective time logging
- Pain Points Addressed: Time Blindness, Task Paralysis
- Etsy Market Gap: Most Etsy offerings are static PDFs/GoodNotes planners; few interactive time visualization tools
- Trend Alignment: High - growing interest in visual time management on Pinterest

### Key Opportunities for MVP
1. **Start with ADHD Task Breaker** - Highest impact/ease score (9/10)
