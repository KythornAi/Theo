## 5. FAQ Points (With Answers)

Based on mined complaints and hesitation patterns:

**Q: Do I need an iPad or GoodNotes?**
A: Nope. Works in any web browser — laptop, phone, tablet, old Chromebook. If it can open a webpage, it works. No app downloads, no file imports.

**Q: Is this a subscription?**
A: No. One payment. One price forever. No monthly fees, no "upgrade to premium."

**Q: I've bought planners before and stopped using them. How is this different?**
A: Fair question. Most planners fail because they require daily maintenance and become another thing to manage. This resets itself. Miss 3 days? 3 weeks? Open it and it's fresh — no backlog, no guilt, no catching up.

**Q: What if I'm having a low-energy day and even this feels like too much?**
A: Click the "Low Energy" toggle and the entire board shrinks to 3 gentle prompts. On really hard days, there's also a "Just Breathe" mode that does nothing but validate you.

**Q: Does this replace my calendar or to-do app?**
A: No. It's your daily reset tool — the thing you use when you're overwhelmed and can't start. Your calendar tells you what's coming; this helps you actually move.

**Q: Can I use this offline?**
A: It needs an internet connection to load. Once open, your session saves locally. If you need offline backup, the printable sheets in your download cover hard days away from screens.

**Q: What's your refund policy?**
A: If you try it and it genuinely doesn't help within 14 days, message us and we'll refund you. No hassle.

## 10. Browser-Based Product Gap (The Differentiator)

The entire Etsy ADHD planner market is **100% file-download based** (PDFs, GoodNotes templates, Canva links). No one sells a browser-based interactive tool. This is both our gap and our risk.

**How to position the gap as a benefit:**

| Market Standard | Our Alternative | How to phrase it |
|----------------|-----------------|------------------|
| Download a PDF → import to GoodNotes → learn the app | Open a URL → start | "No download. No import. Open and go." |
| Works only on iPad/Android with specific apps | Any device, any browser | "Laptop. Phone. Tablet. Whatever's in front of you." |
| Static pages — same every time you open it | Adapts to your energy level | "Reads the room. Shrinks when you're running on empty." |
| Manual entry everywhere | Brain dump auto-sorts into categories | "Dump it all. It sorts the rest." |
| Zero reminders or prompts | Optional gentle nudges | "Can remind you. Won't nag you." |
| Data lost if file crashes or sync fails | Browser-local save + PDF export | "Your stuff stays safe. Export anytime." |
| $17 for a static file | $15.99 for a living tool | "Same price. Actually alive." |
