## Paste-ready product description

If mindfulness has ever sounded useful but also a little impossible, this bundle was made for the real version of your life.

**Mindful, Not Mythical** is a practical ebook about gratitude, calm, self-kindness, attention, emotional steadiness, and everyday clarity. It is written for people who want mindfulness without turning it into a lifestyle performance, a spiritual identity, or another routine to fail at.

The bundle also includes **60 ADHD Daily Affirmation Cards**: printable or digital cards you can keep on a desk, mirror, bedside table, journal, fridge, or phone as visible, low-pressure cues.

Use the ebook when you want a grounded read and a simple practice. Use the cards when you need a quick reminder that your worth is not tied to productivity, perfection, or having a perfectly regulated day.

This is not a planner. It is not a huge workbook. It is a simple self-guided reflection bundle for ordinary life.

### What you get

- **Mindful, Not Mythical** ebook by Carlton Embers
- **60 ADHD Daily Affirmation Cards**
- **Start Here guide** for using the ebook and cards together
- Optional launch bonus if available: **7 Days of Calm & Clarity** mini journal

### Use it when

- you want mindfulness without mysticism or forced positivity
- your brain responds better to visible cues than rigid routines
- you want a kinder reset after a noisy or scattered day
- you need a small reflection practice that can survive imperfect weeks
- you want calm and clarity without needing a retreat, expensive setup, or perfect morning

### This is not for

- medical advice, therapy, diagnosis, or treatment
- crisis support or emergency mental health care
- a full productivity system or planner
- anyone looking for a mystical, manifestation, or retreat-style programme

## FAQ for Gumroad

**Is this a planner?**  
No. It is a practical mindfulness ebook plus a printable affirmation card deck. It can sit alongside a planner, but it is not a productivity system.

**Is this therapy or mental health treatment?**  
No. This is a self-guided reflection resource for personal use. It is not medical advice, therapy, diagnosis, or treatment.

**Do I need to already be into mindfulness?**  
No. The ebook is written for people who may be sceptical of traditional mindfulness language and want something practical, grounded, and ordinary-life friendly.

**Are the cards specifically for ADHD?**  
They are written with ADHD adults in mind: visible, kind, low-pressure prompts rather than rigid instructions or perfection-based routines.

**Can I print the cards?**  
Yes, if the printable PDF export is included. Print results may vary depending on your printer, paper, and settings.

**Can I use the files digitally?**  
The ebook and card files should work in most PDF readers. Only mention GoodNotes, Notability, or fillable features after those exports have been tested.

**Will a physical product be shipped?**  
No. This is a digital download.
