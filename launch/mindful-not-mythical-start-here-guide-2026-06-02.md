## Buyer-facing copy

# Start Here

## Mindful, Not Mythical + 60 ADHD Daily Affirmation Cards

Thank you for picking up **Mindful, Not Mythical**.

This bundle is designed to be simple: one practical ebook, one set of gentle ADHD-aware affirmation cards, and no pressure to turn mindfulness into another perfect routine.

You do not need to read everything in order. You do not need a quiet life, a clear head, or a long morning ritual. Start with the piece that feels easiest today.

---

## What is included

- **Mindful, Not Mythical** — a practical ebook on gratitude, calm, self-kindness, attention, and everyday clarity.
- **60 ADHD Daily Affirmation Cards** — printable or digital cards you can use as visible, low-pressure cues.
- **This Start Here guide** — a quick way to choose your first step.

---

## If you have 5 minutes

Choose one card. Read it once. Put it somewhere you will actually see it: your desk, mirror, bedside table, fridge, journal, phone wallpaper, or inside a notebook.

Let the card be a cue, not a command.

You do not have to believe every word immediately. It can simply remind you to pause, soften the next moment, or speak to yourself with a little less force.

---

## If you have 15 minutes

Open the ebook and choose one chapter that matches today:

- Gratitude when you want perspective without forced positivity.
- Self-kindness when your inner critic is loud.
- Breath or body-based practices when you want a short reset.
- Emotional steadiness when the day feels noisy.
- Connection when you want to feel less alone in ordinary life.

Read one section. Try one small practice. Stop there if that is enough.

---

## If you feel overwhelmed

Try this very small start:

1. Put both feet on the floor if you can.
2. Take one slower breath out.
3. Pick one affirmation card.
4. Ask: “What would make the next few minutes 10% gentler?”

That answer can be tiny. A glass of water. Sitting down. Closing one tab. Asking for help. Lowering the volume of the day.

Tiny counts.

---

## A low-pressure way to use the bundle

You might try a simple weekly rhythm:

- **Monday:** choose one card for the week.
- **Midweek:** read one short ebook section that fits what you are actually dealing with.
- **Weekend:** notice whether any phrase, practice, or card felt useful enough to keep nearby.

If you forget for a week, you have not failed. The bundle is still here when you come back.

This is a self-guided reflection resource for personal use. It is not medical advice, therapy, diagnosis, or treatment. If you are struggling with your mental health or feel unsafe, please seek support from a qualified professional or local crisis service.
