# MTD Checker — Codex Review Brief

## Project
**ReadyForMTD.co.uk** — a UK Making Tax Digital readiness checker for sole traders and landlords. Domain purchased. Research complete. Now deciding on website build approach.

## What we've done
12-file research sprint completed May 2026 covering: HMRC guidance audit, competitor checker analysis, affiliate programmes, audience/keyword research, traffic channel teardown, monetisation map, trust/objections copy, Pinterest/social scan, MVP spec, pre-build audit, domain selection, and lean validation strategy.

Full build brief at: `/home/kylemoore/theo/mtd/build-brief.md`

## What the build brief calls for
- 6 static HTML pages
- Client-side JS eligibility checker (5-7 questions, no email gate)
- Result categories: April 2026 scope (>£50k), April 2027 scope (>£30k), April 2028 scope (>£20k), out of scope, edge cases
- Guide pages for: sole traders, landlords, deadlines, software comparison, setup checklist
- Optional email capture + low-cost setup pack (£7-£15)
- Affiliate links to accounting software
- Mobile-first, static hosting, Google Analytics/Plausible

## Key HMRC timeline (confirmed still current as of June 2026)
- April 2026: MTD for IT mandatory if qualifying income >£50k (2024/25)
- April 2027: Threshold drops to >£30k (2025/26)
- April 2028: Threshold drops to >£20k (2026/27)
